We travel, we eat , we get shocked by the conference, we didn't sleep to much.

The IndySCC25 passed, and it was incredible. We felt like guest of honors, having access to fully conference sponsorship by the comitee of the IndySCC25 was amazing.
We all expected a big conference, but this was other thing. 3 block of pure HPC, with lots of exhibitions, booths showing their technology, workshops and tutorials.

The first day of the conference we start the competition, 46hs to compile and run three applications. We divide the work in three teams.

#### Structural Simulation Toolkit
- Ignacio Facello

#### Exascale Climate Emulators
- Javier Mercado
- Tomas Hubman

#### Mistery App (ACTS)
- Lara Kurtz
- Milagros Carabelos
- Tomas Bazan

The competition didn't result as we expected, but in retrospective we performed quite well, we couldn't compile and run the Mystery App pero seulto que esa fue la gran mayoria de los equipos. Por el resto de los equipos logramos una  performance razonable. Mas informacion en la [nota de UNC Supercomputo](https://supercomputo.unc.edu.ar/2025/11/26/carpinchos-en-indyscc25/)

We’d like to thank, once again to our advisor, Alejandro Silva, for support and the walks between sessions that helped us clear our minds during the competition.

Queda mucho por mejorar, ya estamos enfocados en nuestra proxima competencia, la ASC26 .
